Veggie Goodness

Description

Do you want a pizza in consideration of animal products? No stress, we have something to offer you! The Veggie Goodness Pizza is a vegan and plant based pizza packed with greens and all sorts of vegetables including Basil, Pepper, Mushrooms, Olives, Onions, and some other favourite veggies! This also includes pure and authentic tomato sauce and vegan cheese, that strives to offer the stretchiness of mozzarella! This is the pizza for you if you prefer no animal cruelty or just wants to eat a healthy diet.

(Consideration: Please be aware that this pizza may not be guaranteed to be offered as 100% cruelty-free as it promises in the description due to cross-contamination.)
