Dices of Ham

Description

A simple pizza with tiny dices of ham! But that doesn't mean simplicity is bad enough to ruin the taste! The Dices of Ham Pizza is filled with seasoned diced and smokey hams everywhere evenly on the pizza, the sweet and fresh tomato sauce, and who can not remember that thick and stretchy mozzarella! A basic yet delicious pizza for sure!

(Consideration: We have not directly included pineapple on any of our pizzas for the respect of Italians. For Hawaiian fans, if you'd like, please buy the separate pineapple side dish if you prefer the taste of Hawaiian Pizzas.)
